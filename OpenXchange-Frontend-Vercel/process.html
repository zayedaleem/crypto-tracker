How We Work

A structured engagement model covering both infrastructure delivery and operational management — from first assessment to ongoing operations.

?
01

Assessment

Infrastructure and operations review.

Methodology
Assessment
A full review of your existing infrastructure and operations — mapping gaps across your technology stack, workflows, governance structures, and day-to-day processes. This forms the baseline for everything that follows.
?
02

Design

Infrastructure architecture and operating model design.

Methodology
Design
We design your infrastructure architecture and operating model end to end — defining the systems, controls, approval workflows, and team structures required to run your digital asset environment at institutional standard.
?
03

Vendor & Team Alignment

Technology provider selection and onboarding.

Methodology
Vendor & Team Alignment
We identify and onboard the right technology vendors for your specific needs, and define clear team roles, responsibilities, and accountability structures — ensuring both the technology and the people are aligned before build begins.
?
04

Build & Deploy

Deploying infrastructure, systems, and controls.

Methodology
Build & Deploy
We deploy your infrastructure, systems, and governance controls — and build the full operational documentation alongside it: runbooks, escalation paths, and staff onboarding materials so your team is ready from day one.
?
05

Testing & Validation

End-to-end testing of both infrastructure and operational workflows.

Methodology
Testing & Validation
We run end-to-end testing across both infrastructure and operational workflows — including reconciliation runs, incident simulations, and stress-testing edge cases. Nothing goes live without your formal sign-off.
?
06

Handover & Stabilization

Knowledge transfer, team training, and operational readiness.

Methodology
Handover & Stabilization
We conduct full knowledge transfer and team training, ensuring your environment is stable and operationally ready. If you are moving to our managed operations track, we transition seamlessly into ongoing management from this point.
?
07

Ongoing Managed Operations (optional)

Treasury monitoring, settlement coordination, governance support, reporting.

Methodology
Ongoing Managed Operations (optional)
Once your environment is live, OpenXchange transitions seamlessly into ongoing 24/7 management — covering treasury monitoring, settlement coordination, governance support, reporting, and incident escalation across all time zones.
?
08

Client Onboarding Management (optional)

KYC/AML processes, documentation, identity verification workflows.

Methodology
Client Onboarding Management (optional)
We manage the full client onboarding process on your behalf — KYC/AML documentation, identity verification workflows, risk screening, and approval sign-offs — so your team can focus on growth rather than administration overhead.
